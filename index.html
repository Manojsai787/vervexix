About Verve

The static buzz in the air has been charged into a symphony of restless joy and it is time to find a new rhythm. We're past the days of staring into iridescent screens, it is time to witness grandeur in the flesh, to revive the creativity buried away. The Literary Circle is proud to unveil the official logo for the nineteenth edition of the annual Youth cum Cultural Festival of NIT Durgapur, VERVE. The best weekend of the year awaits you, spread across four enthralling days, from 13th to 17th March, 2024. From a thrilling Treasure Hunt to an enchanting movie screening and a plethora of exciting events in between, Verve XIX is here. Carve the chronicles of your escapade. VERVE XIX. 13th-17th March, 2024.
